Year in Review: 2025

January 12, 2026

2025 was our biggest year yet. We doubled our user base, shipped four major updates, and somehow kept the team small enough to fit around one table.

The highlight was launching our API in March, which opened the door for integrations we never anticipated. By December, over two hundred teams had built on top of it.

Looking ahead to 2026, we're focused on reliability and speed. No flashy features — just making what we have work even better.
