Introducing Our New Dashboard

January 28, 2026

We've completely rebuilt the dashboard from the ground up. The goal was simple: show you what matters and hide everything else.

The new layout loads in under a second, works beautifully on mobile, and puts your most-used actions front and center. We removed twelve settings panels and replaced them with smart defaults that just work.

This update is live for all users today. No migration needed — just open your dashboard and you'll see the change.
