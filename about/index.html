About Minimal Co.

Minimal Co. was founded in 2020 with a single idea: less is more. We believe the best tools are the ones you barely notice — they just work.

Our small team is spread across three time zones, united by a shared love of clean design and clear thinking. We skip the meetings that could be emails and the features that could be bugs.

If that sounds like your kind of company, we'd love to hear from you.
